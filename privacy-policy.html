Privacy Policy for Webs Realty

Dubai's fastest-growing brokerage, setting a new benchmark for exceptional service.

At Webs Realty, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you engage with our services. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information to provide you with tailored services, including but not limited to:

  • ➤ Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and address.
  • ➤ Property Preferences: Information about your preferences for residential or investment properties.
  • ➤ Transactional Data: Information related to property purchases, agreements, or services availed through our partnerships with developers like Emaar, Damac,
    and First Group.
  • ➤ Communication Data: Records of communication, inquiries, and feedback shared with our team.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • ➤ Facilitate property transactions and provide personalized recommendations.
  • ➤ Offer transparent and professional advice to meet your specific needs.
  • ➤ Maintain partnerships with leading developers to deliver exclusive opportunities.
  • ➤ Ensure seamless and rewarding experiences throughout the property acquisition process.
  • ➤ Improve our services and build lasting client relationships.

3. Sharing Your Information

Webs Realty does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following scenarios:

  • ➤ With Partner Developers: To secure properties or offers tailored to your needs.
  • ➤ With Service Providers: To assist in delivering our services (e.g., payment processors, legal advisors).
  • ➤ As Required by Law: To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • ➤ Access and review the information we hold about you.
  • ➤ Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • ➤ Withdraw consent for the use of your information.
  • ➤ Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority if necessary.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. These tools help us analyze usage patterns and tailor our services. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to external websites or services. Webs Realty is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Updates to This Policy

Webs Realty reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be posted on our website with the updated effective date.
